About AYSEF

Building a sustainable platform for ASEAN youth to engage, learn, and innovate in STEM and Economics

Shaping ASEAN's Future Together

AYSEF is a newly founded organization with a shared vision to create lasting opportunities for young people across ASEAN by empowering them to realize their full potential in STEM and Economics.

We provide a unique platform for sharing ideas, experiences, and passions, with insights from industry leaders and academics, following the model of the Youth Economic Forum (YEF) and London International Youth Science Forum (LIYSF).

Regional Collaboration

Foster cooperation among ASEAN youth to address scientific challenges collectively

Regional Innovation

Stimulate discussions for creative solutions in STEM and Economics

Industry Insight

Provide opportunities to learn from and engage with leaders in STEM and economics

Fostering Passions

Cultivate enthusiasm for scientific and economic pursuits among ASEAN youth

About AYSEF

Building a sustainable platform for ASEAN youth to engage, learn, and innovate in STEM and Economics

Shaping ASEAN's Future Together

AYSEF is a newly founded organization with a shared vision to create lasting opportunities for young people across ASEAN by empowering them to realize their full potential in STEM and Economics.

We provide a unique platform for sharing ideas, experiences, and passions, with insights from industry leaders and academics, following the model of the Youth Economic Forum (YEF) and London International Youth Science Forum (LIYSF).
