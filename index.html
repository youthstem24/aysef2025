Our Story

Building Bridges Across ASEAN

AYSEF was born from a powerful vision: to create lasting opportunities for young people across ASEAN by empowering them to realize their full potential in STEM and Economics.

Inspired by the success of the Youth Economic Forum (YEF) and London International Youth Science Forum (LIYSF), we've built a unique platform where ideas flourish, experiences are shared, and passions are ignited through meaningful connections with industry leaders and academics.

Our Mission Pillars

Four foundational principles that guide everything we do at AYSEF

Regional Collaboration

Foster deep cooperation among ASEAN youth to address scientific and economic challenges collectively, building a stronger, more united region.

Innovation & Creativity

Stimulate groundbreaking discussions and creative thinking to develop innovative solutions in STEM and Economics for tomorrow's challenges.

Expert Mentorship

Provide unparalleled opportunities to learn from and engage with industry leaders, policymakers, and academic experts shaping our future.

Igniting Passions

Cultivate genuine enthusiasm and lifelong passion for scientific and economic pursuits among the next generation of ASEAN leaders.
